DATT2500
2015 Portfolio
Selected works:

These rendered images are selected student project results from the DATT2500 introduction to 3D modeling class. The two subjects are still-life and character modeling. For the first project students chose a reference image, such as a painting masterpiece or a photograph they find interesting; for the second, students define and design a character.
The process pipeline includes applying a variety of modeling techniques, applying cinematography including camera, lighting, and shadows, applying shading and texturing, and finally rendering using either local or global illumination.
Nearly all students had no prior experience of 3D modeling or rendering; 95.1% of students were beginner level and 4.9% students were intermediate level: Survey results
